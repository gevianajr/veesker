## Becoming a maintainer

Veesker is in early access. Maintainer roles will be opened to consistent, trusted contributors after the project reaches v1.0 stable. Until then, contributions are welcome via [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md), but maintainer status is reserved.

The criteria when applications open will include:

- A track record of high-quality merged PRs (typically 10+ over 6 months)
- Demonstrated understanding of Oracle Database internals and the Veesker architecture
- Adherence to the Apache 2.0 license terms and the trademark policy
- Sponsor approval from an existing maintainer

## Decision-making

Until the project formally moves to multi-maintainer governance, all final decisions on:

- Releases and version tags
- Architecture changes
- Public roadmap
- Commercial licensing and trademark use
- Code-of-Conduct enforcement

...rest with the Lead Maintainer.

This document will evolve when the project transitions to broader governance.
